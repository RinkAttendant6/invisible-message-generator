What characters can my message contain?

Any character in the first 1023 Unicode code points in the Basic Multilingual Plane. This includes the most common Latin characters (including accented ones), phonetic alphabet characters, and Greek characters.

Notably, emojis and other Unicode characters such as shapes, mathematical symbols, and arrows are not supported.

How do I run the code?

First of all, ensure that you are including the decoding function.
